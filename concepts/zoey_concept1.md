# Zoey: Combat Medic

### Talent 1: Focused Resuscitation
```
+15% Speed While Holding a Health Item Out
-4 Max Health per Level (80 Health)

Improved Revives
- +20% per Level Increased Revive Speed
- Revive Teammates Without Interruption from Infected Hits
- Revived Survivor Gets Normal Health Instead of Temporary Health

Improved Medkit Use
- +20% per Level Increased First Aid Healing Speed
- +4 Health per Level for Target When Healing With Medkits
```

### Talent 2: Trigger Happy
```
Turns Dual Pistols Into Automatic Firing Machine Pistols
- Fast Firing Rate (10 Rounds per Second?)
- +60% Clip Size per Level (120 Max)
- Removes the Ability to Hold a Primary Weapon

Press [WALK] + [RELOAD] to Activate Explosive Pistols Mode
- Machine Pistol Bullets Explode 
- Kills All CI with One Shot
- Increased Damage to SI
- Reloads Current Clip 
    - All Ammo Converted Until the Clip Runs Out
- Last 30 seconds
- 30 Second Cooldown
```

### Talent 3: Mop 'Til They Drop
```
Press [WALK] + [USE] To Activate
Then Shoot Target to Activate Mop The Floor

Mop The Floor:
- Automically Kill Common Infected Nearest to Shot Target
- Kills Number of CI Equal to the Mop The Floor's Charge
- Charge Mop The Floor with Machine Pistol Hits on CI and SI
    - 5 Infected Hits = 1 Charge
```

### Talent 6: Instant Intervention
```
+5% Speed Increase for Each Downed Survivor (+15% Max)

Hold [WALK] to Teleport to Chosen Survivor's location
- 5 seconds of 50% Damage Reduction After Teleport
- Drops a Healing Circle On Teleport Location
    - Heals 5 HP per Second to Everyone In Circle
    - 5 Second Duration
- Cant Be Used While Being Grappled
- 3 Minute Global Cooldown
- Globally Limited to 3 Uses Per Round
```

Drops Medical Support Kit On Targeted Player
    - Select Player and Item From Menu
    - Player Must Be Visible to You
    - 30 Second Cooldown After Drop
Zoey Starts With 3 Medical Support Kits
Maximum of 3 Kits At Once
Also Gained by Scavenging Extra Supplies
    - 1 Medkit
    - 2 Pill Bottles
    - 2 Adrenaline Shots
    
Menu Items for Targeted Player (Zoey Picks):
    - 10 Seconds of 50% Damage Reduction
    - +1 Bile Cleansing Kit
    - +1 Adrenaline
    - +1 Pill Bottle
    - Remove Black and White Status
        - Limit of 3 per Round
        - 2 Minute Cooldown
