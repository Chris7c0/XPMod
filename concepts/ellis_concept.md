# Ellis: Weapons Expert

### Talent 1: Overconfidence
```
Max Health Reduced by 10 HP
Max Temp Health is 125 HP
Pills Only Heal 15 HP
Start With An Extra Shot

If Within 40 Points Of Max Health:
- +1% Movement Speed
- +5% RoF To All Guns per Level

While On Adrenaline:
- +5 Temp Health per Level
- +10% RoF To All Guns per Level
- (Team) +2 Seconds Duration per Level
  - Stacks with every Ellis
```

### Talent 2: Bring the Pain!
```
All Health Is Converted To Temp Health

Start With A Self Revive Kit

+1 Adrenaline Shot on Self Revive Kit Use

On Special Infected kill:
- Regen +1 Temp Health per Level
- +8 Clip Ammo per Level
- +1% Movement Speed (Stacks)
  - +2 Max Stacks per Level
```

### Talent 3: Jammin' to the Music
```
Stash Up To 3 Extra Adrenaline Shots
- Stash Up To 5 On Tank Spawned Shots

On Tank Spawn:

Level 1:
+1% Movement Speed per Level

Level 5:
+1 Adrenaline Shot
+1 Molotov
```

### Talent 4: Weapons Training
```
Level 1:
+15% Reload Speed per Level
(Team) +8% Laser Accuracy per Level

Level 5:
Automatic Laser Sight
Ellis Can Carry 2 Primary Weapons
[WALK+ZOOM] To Cycle Weapons
```

### Talent 5: Mechanic Affinity
```
Level 1:
Can No Longer Hold Melee Weapons
+5% Firing Rate per Level
All Guns Are Automatic Now
- Excludes Snipers

Level 5:
[WALK+USE] Triple Firing Rate for 5 Seconds
- Destroys Weapon After

Bind 1: Ammo Refill
- +1 Use Every Other Level

Deploy An Ammo Stash
Instantly Refill All Survivors Ammo
```

### Talent 6: Fire Storm
```
Ellis Can Carry and Starts With +1 Molotov
+10 Clip Size per Level (SMG/Rifle/Sniper Only)
Fire Immunity

Bind 2: Summon Kagu-Tsuchi's Wrath
- +1 Use Every Other Level
- +1 Molotov on Use

Temporarily Grants Ellis Incendiary Attacks With All Weapons
- +3 Sec Duration Added per Level

Burning A Calm Witch Immediately Neutralizes Her
```
