# Nick: Gambler

### Talent 1: Swindler
```
Level 1:
+3 max health per level when ally uses a kit (max +100)
+1 life stealing recovery every other level
+1 life stealing damage every level

Skill Uses:
- Life stealing ticks every second for 5 seconds
- Life stealing only affects SI
```

### Talent 2: Leftover Supplies
```
Level 1:
+15% chance to spawn items when you use a medkit per level

Level 5:
Press [ZOOM] with a kit out to destroy it and gain:
- 1 random weapon
- 1 random grenade, 1 shot, or 1 pill
```

### Talent 3: Risky Business
```
All upgrades are (p220 & Glock):

Level 1:
+20% reload speed per level
+20% damage per level
+6 clip size per level

Level 5:
Pistols are now automatic
Press [WALK+ZOOM] cycle to dual pistols
You can cycle back to Magnums
```

### Talent 4: Enhanced Pain Killers
```
Level 1:
+6 temp health per level from pills & shots
Recover +1 health per level when anyone uses shots & pills (+8 at max)

Shoot Survivors with Pistols to heal them...at a cost.
- Pistols: +2 HP for Teammate, -1 HP for You
- Magnum: +7 HP for Teammate, -3 HP for You
```

### Talent 6: Desperate Measures
```
Level 1:
(Stacks) +2% speed & +10% gun damage per level
```
