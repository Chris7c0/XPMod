Why So Serious?:
Every punch squeaks. Due to the excruciatingly annoying sound, uncommons & commons deal +1 damage per Circus King punch up to +3 because clowns are annoying. Common speed also increases by 1 per hit. However, they’re afraid of The King, so they choose to take it out on survivors. Lasts throughout the match (erm?)

MY CLOWNDOM:

Loses 9% (max or current?) hp per Super Clown killed, but +6.9% speed, and +3 damage per slain Super Clown
Super Clowns: 
Not immune to fire, but heavily resistant and heat causes them to multiply into normal clowns, which quickly extinguish flames as they multiply. 
CONFETTI CRANIUM!!: If headshotted, they explode into a pool of mini clowns of random enhancement. 
Ignores pipe/bile bombs. 
 Deal 10 damage and spawns 2 normal clowns a fair distance away per hit.
After hitting a survivor, has a 50% chance to make a survivor equip a gnome and drop their item the next time they swap equipment. 
Also has the Why So Serious? Passive, just like the Circus King. Except they can each only add +1 damage to commons and +1 speed.

Super clowns are massive but gradually shrink to tiny size upon being hit and may even run away, similar to the Survivor Uncommon Infected. They only run away when damaged, and are aggressive otherwise, similar to the Fallen Survivor. Melee weapons kill them in one hit, but they are highly resistant to bullet damage. Noticed by being highlighted in gold. 

If Noob Wipe is used when a Super Clown is alive, instead jump scares all survivors  and turns all commons into clowns.

Or instead, two clowns spawn every 3 seconds. 

Indoctrinates nearby Commons into the Circus Society, unofficially-clownficially bringing them into Eldritch Clownhood. Commons within 67 feet of the Circus King have a 69% chance of being changed into Eldritch Clownhood Enlistees every 4 seconds. If a common is not transformed from the first 67% roll, they are ineligible for being transformed again.
Eldritch Clownhood Enlistee: Clown model, Vampiric and Necromantic variants. Rare chance(5%) of a common becoming  Jerry The Jester:
Jerry The Jester: 
Jimmy Gibbs durability
Applies random enhancement effect on hit(Ice, Necrofeaster, Fire, Vamp)
When damaged, becomes a random type of enhancement (this can stack with the previous enhancement)
When damaged: 10% chance for a mini clown to spawn around it
On death: leaves a cloud of laughing gas which increases screenshake by 15% per second a survivor is within it


CLOWN FLOWER: Takes x2 damage from melee weapons, but squeaks when meleed, permanently increasing the sprint speed and damage of common/uncommon infected per received melee hit. It also utilizes its squirting flower to splash the attacking survivor upon being meleed, blinding them and causing the survivor to slip. Shoving the tank will have the same effect (not sure why you’re shoving a tank but have fun, bud)

Right click: Throws a Laughing Jockey Ball. Depending on where the Laughing Jockey Ball lands, the following with occur:

Type A: If the Laughing Jockey Ball does not hit a survivor, only colliding with a surface: Throws a bundle of jockeys and clowns that stay in an area wherever they land. 
The jockey-clown mass cannot move or attack. 

They are also not tangible; survivors can walk through them

The mass does no damage to survivors, but applies debuffs (below)

The jockey-clown mass has 420 health 

Cannot be damaged by melee weapons, but can be shot or blown up (pipe bombs, grenade launchers, propane/air tanks; dies in one hit from explosions)

If ignited, the mass will die instantly but they will let out a final, excruciatingly loud laugh which summons a horde of clowns.  

If the survivors walk through this circle of jockey-clowns, they will be slowed very slightly, have their visions be severely disoriented; spinning it, increasing their shove fatigue, and receive increased screen shake for the time in the jockey-clown circle and for 5 seconds after. Commons will also instantly gain aggression to them, singling them out (remove if it turns out to be more detrimental than a benefit to the tank)

Type B: If the Laughing Jockey Ball hits a survivor: A Jockey jumpscares all survivors making them take 3 damage
The survivor hit in particular will take typical rock-throw damage, have their screenshake increased tremendously, make them randomly equip a gnome 2-17 seconds later and the item in their explosive slot will instantly detonate. 

The following will occur depending on the dropped explosive item:

Molotov: All Un/Commons(those that are not CEDA) gain burn immunity for a duration

Pipe Bomb: Squeaks, causes a clown horde event, and gives the survivor a gnome
Bile Jar: Biles the survivor and those nearby

Can only be a bot
Follows the same Laughing Jockey Ball throw rules as the Circus King
Has CLOWN FLOWER passive
Has Eldritch Clownhood passive
Does not have any passives of the Circus King not mentioned
+30% speed
		
		THE CLOWN CAR CRASHED… AGAIN!!:
		On death of the Clown Tank, the desperate Circus King’s soul within it cries out, and its body explodes with a Mighty Honk. 

	Mighty Honk: 
Stumbles survivors nearby from a propane tank-like explosion (replaced with a squeak noise).
Infected pour out from its body; a random assortment of common & uncommon infected run out of its corpse, up to 15 over three seconds. Additionally, three Jackass Jockeys and 3 Kamiklownzies


Jackass Jockeys: High speed, can leap & scratch but falls off immediately upon hopping onto someone and dies, dealing 6 damage through infectious mania over three seconds (2 damage per second, forces survivors to laugh, attracting commons to them for the duration.)

Kamiklownzie: Random in size, though most likely to be normal. Explodes on death, shove, or if the Kamiklownize successfully lands a hit. Must be shot to avoid explosion.
